ABOUT US — 01

BORN IN BERLIN.
RAISED ON real FOOD.

VitaPod was founded in Berlin in 2026 on a simple conviction: grabbing food on the go should never mean settling. So we built our own kitchen, hired real chefs, and put their work behind smart glass — fresh, fast and genuinely good, around the clock.

Every bowl, bar and bottle is made with real ingredients by our own team — then stocked into smart pods where you actually are: gyms, campuses, coworking spaces and stations across the city.

Make real, chef-quality food as accessible as a vending machine.

FAQ

GOOD QUESTIONS.

How fresh is the food, really?+
Everything is made in our Berlin kitchen and stocked daily before 7:00. Cold items are eaten within their fresh window; hot meals are flash-frozen at peak and reheated on demand in the pod.
How do I pay?+
Two ways. Easiest is the app — scan to unlock, take what you want, close the door, and the bill settles automatically with your loyalty points landing instantly. Prefer not to use the app? Tap your VitaPod membership card and pay contactless with NFC right at the pod.
Are there vegan and high-protein options?+
Plenty of both. Most drinks carry 22–30g of protein, meals 38–42g, and a large share of the shelf — bowls, mocktails, chocolate and tapas — is fully plant-based.
Where can I find a pod?+
We're launching across Berlin with pods at Mitte Gym & Spa, HWR Berlin Campus, WeWork Kreuzberg, Berlin Hbf and East Side Fitness — with FU Berlin Mensa opening soon and new locations every month. The Find Us map and the app always show what's nearest and open.
